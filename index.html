REAL-TIME FOG REMOVAL SYSTEM AND ITS METHOD THEREOF

The present invention provides a visible and near-infrared band's deep learning and pixel true up-based system and method for image and video dehazing. It is used to restore haze/foggy images with a wider range of hazing conditions. Unlike the prior art, the proposed invention utilized a wide electromagnetic spectrum for imaging from 430nm to 1000nm wavelength and performed dehazing/defogging in the entire range in visible and NIR bands.
